Help & FAQ
What is Comedy Mastermind?
Comedy Mastermind is an AI comedy coach built from Scott Dikkers' complete body of work — his books, courses, speeches, and decades of teaching. It helps you brainstorm ideas, improve jokes, get feedback on your writing, and build a comedy career.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
Comedy Mastermind is trained exclusively on Scott Dikkers' frameworks and philosophy. It uses his specific terminology, his 11 Funny Filters, and his approach to comedy craft — not generic AI responses.
Will it write jokes for me?
It can generate raw material, but its real value is making your comedy better. It's a coach, not a ghostwriter. The goal is to develop your judgment and voice.
Can I save my conversations?
Yes — your conversations are automatically saved and can be renamed and revisited anytime from the Conversation History menu. For best results, start a new conversation when switching to a new topic or project.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from the Subscription screen in this menu. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
